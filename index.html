Welcome to a Master Talent Hunt

Master Talent Hunt is an online logical aptitude test, being conducted to identify the best talents from every nook and corners of India, and duly reward them so that they could live up to the potential, and realize their dreams.

The competition is open to participation for students from Class-1 to Class-12, and involves an online, objective-type, mental-ability test, normalized for the class of the student. Since it is meant to test the inherent talent and abilities of the participants, it does not call for any extra or special preparation.
